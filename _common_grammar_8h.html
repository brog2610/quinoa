Detailed Description

Generic, low-level grammar, re-used by specific grammars.

Author:
J. Bakosi
Date:
Mon 01 Jun 2015 02:02:39 PM MDT
Copyright:
2012-2015, Jozsef Bakosi.

Generic, low-level grammar. We use the [Parsing Expression Grammar Template Library (PEGTL)](https://code.google.com/p/pegtl/wiki/PEGTL0) to create the grammar and the associated parser. Credit goes to Colin Hirsch (pegtl@cohi.at) for PEGTL.
