00007   \brief     System of diagonal Ornstein-Uhlenbeck SDEs
00008   \details   This file implements the time integration of a system of stochastic
00009     differential equations (SDEs), with linear drift and constant diagonal
00010     diffusion, whose invariant is the joint [normal
00011     distribution](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Normal_distribution).
00012 
00013     In a nutshell, the equation integrated governs a set of scalars,
00014     \f$Y_\alpha\f$, \f$\alpha\!=\!1,\dots,N\f$, as
00015     \f[
00016        \mathrm{d}Y_\alpha(t) = \theta_\alpha\left(\mu_\alpha - Y_\alpha\right)
00017        \mathrm{d}t + \sigma_\alpha\mathrm{d}W_\alpha(t), \qquad \alpha=1,\dots,N
00018     \f]
00019     with parameter vectors \f$\theta_\alpha > 0\f$, \f$\mu_\alpha\f$, and
00020     \f$\sigma_\alpha > 0\f$. Here \f$\mathrm{d}W_\alpha(t)\f$ is an isotropic
00021     vector-valued [Wiener process](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wiener_process)
00022     with independent increments. The invariant distribution is the joint normal
00023     distribution. This system of SDEs consists of N independent equations, each
00024     being a single-variate [Ornstein-Uhlenbeck
00025     process](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ornstein%E2%80%93Uhlenbeck_process).
00026 
00027     From the Fokker-Planck equation, equivalent to the SDE above, the equations
00028     governing the means, \f$ \langle Y_\alpha \rangle\f$, are
00029     \f[
00030       \newcommand{\irmean}[1]{{\langle{#1}\rangle}}
00031       \frac{\partial\irmean{Y_\alpha}}{\partial t} =
00032         \theta_\alpha\left(\mu_\alpha - \irmean{Y_\alpha}\right)
00033     \f]
00034     while the equation governing the covariance matrix, \f$ \langle y_\alpha
00035     y_\beta \rangle \equiv \left\langle (Y_\alpha - \langle Y_\alpha \rangle)
00036     (Y_\beta - \langle Y_\beta\rangle) \right\rangle \f$, is
00037     \f[
00038       \newcommand{\irmean}[1]{{\langle{#1}\rangle}}
00039       \newcommand{\irv}[1]{\langle{#1^2}\rangle}
00040       \frac{\partial\irmean{y_\alpha y_\beta}}{\partial t} =
00041         -\left(\theta_\alpha+\theta_\beta\right)\irmean{y_\alpha y_\beta} +
00042         \left\{ \begin{array}{lr}
00043           \sigma_\alpha^2 & \mathrm{for} \quad \alpha = \beta,\\
00044           0               & \mathrm{for} \quad \alpha \ne \beta
00045         \end{array} \right..
00046     \f]
00047 */
