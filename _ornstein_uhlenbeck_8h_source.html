00007   \brief     System of Ornstein-Uhlenbeck SDEs
00008   \details   This file implements the time integration of a system of stochastic
00009     differential equations (SDEs), with linear drift and constant diffusion,
00010     whose invariant is the [joint normal
00011     distribution](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multivariate_normal_distribution).
00012 
00013     In a nutshell, the equation integrated governs a set of scalars,
00014     \f$Y_\alpha\f$, \f$\alpha\!=\!1,\dots,N\f$, as
00015     \f[
00016        \mathrm{d}Y_\alpha(t) = \theta_\alpha\left(\mu_\alpha - Y_\alpha\right)
00017        \mathrm{d}t + \sum_{\beta=1}^N \sigma_{\alpha\beta}\mathrm{d}W_\beta(t),
00018        \qquad \alpha=1,\dots,N
00019     \f]
00020     with parameter vectors \f$\theta_\alpha > 0\f$, \f$\mu_\alpha\f$, and
00021     symmetric positive semi-definite diffusion matrix
00022     \f$\sigma_{\alpha\beta}\f$. Here \f$\mathrm{d}W_\beta(t)\f$ is an isotropic
00023     vector-valued [Wiener process](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wiener_process)
00024     with independent increments. The invariant distribution is the joint normal
00025     distribution. This system of SDEs consists of N coupled equations, each
00026     being a single-variate [Ornstein-Uhlenbeck
00027     process](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ornstein%E2%80%93Uhlenbeck_process).
00028 
00029     From the Fokker-Planck equation, equivalent to the SDE above, the equations
00030     governing the means, \f$ \langle Y_\alpha \rangle\f$, are
00031     \f[
00032       \newcommand{\irmean}[1]{{\langle{#1}\rangle}}
00033       \frac{\partial\irmean{Y_\alpha}}{\partial t} =
00034         \theta_\alpha\left(\mu_\alpha - \irmean{Y_\alpha}\right)
00035     \f]
00036     while the equation governing the covariance matrix, \f$ \langle y_\alpha
00037     y_\beta \rangle \equiv \left\langle (Y_\alpha - \langle Y_\alpha \rangle)
00038     (Y_\beta - \langle Y_\beta\rangle) \right\rangle \f$, is
00039     \f[
00040       \newcommand{\irmean}[1]{{\langle{#1}\rangle}}
00041       \newcommand{\irv}[1]{\langle{#1^2}\rangle}
00042       \frac{\partial\irmean{y_\alpha y_\beta}}{\partial t} =
00043       -\left(\theta_\alpha+\theta_\beta\right)\irmean{y_\alpha y_\beta}
00044       +\sum_{\gamma=1}^N \sigma_{\alpha\gamma} \sigma_{\gamma\beta}.
00045     \f]
00046 */
